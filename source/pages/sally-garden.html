Sally Garden

at Good Note Community Farm

Why a Sally Garden?

  • Give us a reliable source of cuttings in Alberta so our members can plant and grow there own willow for weaving
  • Give members who do not have the ability to grow there own willow access to cultivated willow.
  • Create a supply of willow for workshops where the instructor is coming from out of town.

How can you help?

Right now we need help keeping the willow watered. The willow cuttings are just getting settled into their new home and should be watered twice a week.

Maryann suggests you plan to spend 2 hrs watering the willow.

You will be compinsated for your time in willow. When the Sally Garden starts producing we will divy up the willow so that each person will get an allotment of willow based on the hours volunteered.

Take a look at the calendar bellow. Pick a day that works for you, and then use the appointment booker to let us know when you will be coming out. Once you are done watering the willow use this google form to log your hours.
